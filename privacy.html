Soulforge Knight — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

Soulforge Knight is published by Support Ethics Ltd (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what information the game handles.

The short version

  • The game has no accounts and no sign-in. We never see your name, email address, or contacts.
  • Your save (progress, unlocks, settings) is stored only on your device. Deleting the app deletes it.
  • The game shows ads and offers optional purchases. The companies that provide those services process limited device data, described below.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The game shows optional rewarded ads and occasional interstitial ads via Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may process: device identifiers (such as the advertising ID), IP address, approximate location derived from it, and ad-interaction data. In the UK/EEA you will be shown a consent prompt (Google UMP) on first launch and can choose non-personalized ads. On iOS, the App Tracking Transparency prompt lets you decline tracking entirely. You can reset your advertising ID or limit ad tracking in your device settings at any time. Google’s policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Purchasing Remove Ads permanently disables interstitial ads.

Purchases (Apple / Google / RevenueCat)

In-app purchases are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play; we never see your payment details. We use RevenueCat to validate purchases and restore them across reinstalls; it processes anonymized purchase receipts and a random app-scoped identifier. RevenueCat’s policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

Children

The game is a fantasy battle game suitable for ages 9+ and is not directed at children under 13. It contains no chat, no user-generated content, and no data collection beyond the advertising and purchase processing described above.

Your choices & contact

  • Decline or withdraw ad consent via the in-app prompt (UK/EEA), the iOS tracking prompt, or your device’s ad settings.
  • Remove all local data by uninstalling the app.
  • Questions or requests: sean@support-ethics.co.uk

We will update this policy if the game’s data practices change; material changes will be noted in the app’s store listing.
